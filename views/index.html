WHY CHOOSE BROSNAN?

Brosnan’s Global Security Operations Center (GSOC) is an integral part of our Smart Security Solutions, assisting with and sustaining the totality of service delivery and operations.

Our nationwide network of security guards gives you immediate access to highly trained professionals. Brosnan’s dispatch team is standing by 24/7/365 to fulfill permanent, temporary and emergency personnel needs. By tapping into our network, you can flex your staffing as you grow or for special events and emergencies.

Brosnan has deep relationships with law enforcement at the local and national levels, giving our clients an added layer of protection. Founded by former NYPD detective Patrick Brosnan, our company prioritizes communicating with law enforcement at every level. The Brosnan Command Center tracks law enforcement scanners and alerts and coordinates with the appropriate officials when incidents affect your organization.

The Brosnan Command Center is the tactical differentiator which monitors, collects, analyzes and disseminates data from multiple sources to on-site personnel, ensuring client assets and property are effectively secured. The BCC leverages our patented security solution and monitors, collects, analyses, and disseminates data to our security officers in the field in over 1000 sites across America. Our Command Center gives clients real-time visibility into guard activities and incident reporting, providing complete transparency. We make it easy to view and manage your security program. Every officer is equipped with a smartphone that communicates with the Brosnan Command Center. From timekeeping to alerts and incident reports, your data is surfaced in a customizable dashboard.

Our 24/7 operations team is always working to ensure service levels are met. Account managers and supervisors use site visits, officer training, Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and regular client calls to provide unparalleled service. In addition, Brosnan senior management monitors your account and reaches out regularly to confirm your satisfaction.
